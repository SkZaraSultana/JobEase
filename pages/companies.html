Company Insights by Branch

Top Companies & Insights for Freshers

1️⃣ Computer Science & Engineering (CSE / IT)

Top Companies for Freshers:

  • Google – Software Engineer Intern / New Grad
  • Microsoft – SDE Intern / Associate
  • Amazon – Software Development Engineer
  • TCS – IT Trainee / Developer
  • Infosys – Systems Engineer / Developer
  • Wipro – Software Engineer Trainee

Other Notable Companies:

  • Adobe, IBM, Oracle, Cisco, SAP

Useful Insights:

  • Roles include Web/Full Stack, App Development, Data Science, AI/ML.
  • Freshers packages vary ₹3–6 LPA depending on company and skills.
  • Strong coding and problem-solving skills help in product-based companies.

2️⃣ Electrical Engineering

Top Companies for Freshers:

  • Siemens – Electrical Engineer / Automation
  • ABB – Power Systems / Electrical Design
  • BHEL – Trainee Engineer / Electrical
  • Schneider Electric – Automation / Controls
  • TCS – Electrical & Automation roles

Other Notable Companies:

  • GE, L&T Electrical, Crompton Greaves, Reliance Energy

Useful Insights:

  • Focus on power systems, embedded systems, and PLC programming.
  • Internships in automation and robotics are highly valuable.

3️⃣ Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)

Top Companies for Freshers:

  • Intel – Embedded Systems / Firmware Developer
  • Texas Instruments – IC Design / Embedded Systems
  • Qualcomm – Communications / IoT Development
  • Infosys / TCS – Embedded & IoT Projects
  • Cisco – Network / Telecommunication roles

Other Notable Companies:

  • Analog Devices, Bosch, Samsung Electronics, Wipro

Useful Insights:

  • Strong knowledge of embedded systems, microcontrollers, IoT is valued.
  • Roles may include Firmware, Network Engineer, Field Service Engineer.

4️⃣ Civil Engineering

Top Companies for Freshers:

  • L&T Construction – Site Engineer / Project Engineer
  • Reliance Infrastructure – Civil Design / Planning
  • Shapoorji Pallonji – Structural Engineer / Site Engineer
  • Tata Projects – Junior Engineer / Planner

Other Notable Companies:

  • HCC, NCC, Gammon India, Engineers India Ltd.

Useful Insights:

  • Strong CAD and project management skills are advantageous.
  • Construction and structural design internships add value.

5️⃣ Mechanical Engineering

Top Companies for Freshers:

  • Maruti Suzuki – Design / Production Engineer
  • Tata Motors – Mechanical / Automotive Engineer
  • Mahindra & Mahindra – Mechanical Design / Production
  • Larsen & Toubro – Mechanical / Maintenance Engineer
  • Bosch – Automation / Mechatronics roles

Other Notable Companies:

  • GE, Hyundai, Siemens, Cummins

Useful Insights:

  • Focus on CAD, manufacturing processes, and automation skills.
  • Internships in design, production, or robotics are beneficial.
